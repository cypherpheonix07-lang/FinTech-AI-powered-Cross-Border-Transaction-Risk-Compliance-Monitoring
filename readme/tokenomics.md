# 🪙 PathGuard: Web3 & Tokenomics
## *Decentralized Verification & Incentivized Integrity*

### 1. Vision for Decentralized Trust
PathGuard extends the traditional financial rails by integrating Web3 technologies to provide a multi-layered verification ecosystem. We envision a future where transaction validation is shared across a decentralized network of nodes, ensuring zero single-point-of-failure and cryptographic certainty.

---

### 2. Token Utility ($GUARD)
The **$GUARD** token serves as the fuel for the PathGuard verification network:

- **Staking**: Nodes must stake $GUARD to join the verification network, ensuring "skin in the game".
- **Verification Rewards**: Nodes are rewarded in $GUARD for successful and timely transaction path validations.
- **Governance**: Token holders participate in protocol upgrades and regulatory adaptation voting.
- **Payment Layer**: Banks and institutions pay verification fees in $GUARD (or fiat converted to $GUARD).

---

### 3. Tokenomics Model
| Metric | Distribution | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Network Rewards** | 40% | Long-term incentivization for verification nodes. |
| **Ecosystem Fund** | 20% | Grants for new bank integrations and developer tools. |
| **Team & Advisors** | 15% | Multi-year vesting for the core builders. |
| **Public Sale** | 15% | Initial liquidity and community distribution. |
| **Reserve** | 10% | Emergency liquidity and strategic partnerships. |

---

### 4. Deflationary Mechanisms
- **Verification Burn**: A percentage of every transaction fee is permanently burned from the supply.
- **Penalty Slashing**: Tokens from malicious or failing nodes are slashed and returned to the ecosystem fund or burned.

---

### 5. Roadmap to Decentralization
1. **Phase 1**: Centralized GNN Risk Modeling (Current).
2. **Phase 2**: Federated Verification (Selected institutional partners).
3. **Phase 3**: Permissionless Verification Layer (Full Web3 integration).
